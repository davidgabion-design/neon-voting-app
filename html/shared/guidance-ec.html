EC Guidance & Information

Everything you need to know as an Election Commissioner on the Neon Voting System.

Your Role as Election Commissioner

As an EC, you are responsible for creating, configuring, and managing elections within your organization. Your work is reviewed by Super Admin before voting begins to ensure accuracy and fairness.

How the Election Process Works

  1. Login - Use your Organization ID and EC password to access your dashboard
  2. Set Up Election - Add positions, candidates, and voters for your election
  3. Submit for Approval - When ready, submit your election to Super Admin for review
  4. Make Corrections (if needed) - If your election is returned, fix issues and resubmit
  5. Monitor Voting - Once approved, voting opens automatically and you can track turnout
  6. View Results - After voting ends, review outcomes and declare results publicly

Features Available to You

  • Voters Tab - Add voters individually or in bulk (CSV/Excel)
  • Positions Tab - Define election positions and vote limits
  • Candidates Tab - Add candidates with photos and taglines
  • Invites Tab - Send voter credentials via email, SMS, or WhatsApp
  • Outcomes Tab - View real-time results and declare winners
  • Settings Tab - Configure organization details and election type
  • Approval Tab - Submit elections and track approval status
