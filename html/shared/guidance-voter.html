Voter Guidance & Information

Everything you need to know to cast your vote securely and confidently.

How Voting Works

  1. Login - Use your Organization ID and the email/phone you were registered with
  2. Review Candidates - See all candidates for each position with their photos and details
  3. Cast Your Vote - Select your preferred candidates (follow vote limits for each position)
  4. Confirm - Review your selections and submit your ballot
  5. Vote Recorded - Your vote is encrypted and securely stored
  6. Check Results - After voting closes, log in again to view published results

What to Expect

Before Voting Opens:

  • You may receive an invitation email, SMS, or WhatsApp message with your credentials
  • If you don't receive credentials, contact your Election Commissioner

During Voting:

  • Voting is open for a specific time period - don't miss it!
  • You can log in, review candidates, and take your time before submitting
  • Once you submit, your vote is final and cannot be changed

After Voting:

  • You can log back in to see that your vote was recorded
  • Wait for the Election Commissioner to declare results
  • View official results transparently when published

Security & Privacy Guarantee

Neon Voting System uses industry-standard encryption to protect your vote. Your ballot is completely anonymous and cannot be traced back to you. All votes are stored securely and counted accurately.
