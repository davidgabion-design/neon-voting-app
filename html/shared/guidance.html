What is Neon Voting System?

Neon Voting System is a secure, auditable, and multi-tenant digital election platform built for institutions, associations, and organizations that value transparency and trust.

How the System Works

  1. Organizations register and get assigned an Election Commissioner (EC)
  2. ECs create and configure elections with positions, candidates, and voters
  3. Super Admin reviews and approves elections before voting begins
  4. Once approved, voting opens securely to eligible voters
  5. Votes are encrypted, anonymous, and tamper-proof
  6. Results are published transparently after voting ends
